How to use the calculator

Using this calculator is very easy! First, locate the select pool shape section, there select the shape you want to work with. Then enter the dimensions of the figure and click the calculate button. And that's it, all that's left to do is watch the preview of your figure in the black area below and its information fill in the statistics table.

Additional information

Some things to keep in mind are:

  • All numbers must be in meters
  • When resizing it assumes the original depth values and is unaffected by changes in the input, until you click the calculate button again
  • The shape can only be moved and resized within the specified area. If you leave the white frame with your mouse, the action will stop

There are many reasons you may need to know how many liters of water your pool holds. This is useful for knowing how much of a certain chemical to add to your pool. It is also necessary for certain treatment programs, such as dealing with stains or algae.

Knowing the size of your pool is a must for any new equipment purchases as well. Whether it is a new pump or a pool heater, you will need to calculate the size of the equipment to see if it's right for your pool. Fortunately, it's not hard to calculate how many liters are in your pool!
